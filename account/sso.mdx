<Note>
This feature is only available to Enterprise customers, on our Cloud platform. See our [Enterprise Plan](https://novu.co/pricing) to learn more.
</Note>

Novu offers a SAML SSO integration that allows you to authenticate your organization users with your existing SAML infrastructure.

Novu Supports any SAML 2.0 compliant IdP, including:
- Okta Workspace
- Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD)
- Google Workspaces
- Any Other SAML 2.0 compliant IdP

To enable SAML SSO for your account, contact your account manager or [sales@novu.co](mailto:sales@novu.co).
